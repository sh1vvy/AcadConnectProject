About

Who we are

A common knowledge platform (with a facility for plagiarism) to bring all project works taken up at various levels by the students in Technical / Higher Educational Institutes and Universities throughout the country , it will be a great source of knowledge and also will help the student community to take up unique/innovative project works.

  • Can search projects and researches under one platform

  • Auto Recommendation Software

  • Project linker option (collaborative work)

  • Partial Plagiarism for idea and research.

Can Search projects and researches under one platform

  • A space for combined progress and contrast knowledge.
  • Enable students to interact, enact and research on their academic work.
  • Awareness and collaborations on academic works such as projects, research papers and more.

It provides Competitive space and Collaborations for students. The information on this platform will help in the peer-to-peer learning and will help in cross functional research between various universities/colleges.

Auto recommends for better results

  • Auto-Recommender system is being developed with the help of azure machine learning.
  • It gives an edge to the students for getting smooth results and finding better matches for their work.
  • Provides accurate results for research models after learning from the user.

We have a feature based on machine learning which eases the work of students and helps them to have much more options recommended by the software for more accurate results.

Flexibility to link projects

  • Project linker feature makes it possible for two individuals to make a project and research with each other.
  • This feature also makes it possible for an easier flow of idea around the community.
  • Linking two individuals makes it easy for research and projects to execute with great vision and idea.

Project linker is a very unique feature that AcadConnect adds to its space. It is the USP of the application that makes it unique.

Protecting uniqueness of projects

  • It allows betterment of existing models and research.
  • It makes it easy and viable for many projects and research to gather information.
  • It is beneficial for the creator if his credits are added onto another research and model.

Partial plaigairism for an idea is a feature that AcadConnect provides for free to students. It is only for purpose of increasing knowledge base of students.
